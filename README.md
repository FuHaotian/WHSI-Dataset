# WHSI-Weihai-Signalized-Intersection-Dataset
WHSI utilized a drone (DJI Mavic-3) to capture a video of a signal-controlled intersection in Weihai, China. The video was captured at a window size of 1920Ã—1080 and a frequency of 30Hz. The shooting took place from 3:00 pm to 5:30 pm on October 12, 2024, spanning a total of 150 minutes. The length of the intersection's lanes captured was approximately 300 meters.
